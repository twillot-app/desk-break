# Exercise data & media notice

desk-break's no-equipment exercises (names, body parts, and step-by-step
instructions) are derived from the **[hasaneyldrm/exercises-dataset](https://github.com/hasaneyldrm/exercises-dataset)**,
whose **data** is released under the **MIT License**. The bundled files
`i18n/<lang>/exercises.tsv` are a filtered (equipment = "body weight"), reshaped
subset of that data, generated by `tools/build-exercises.py`.
